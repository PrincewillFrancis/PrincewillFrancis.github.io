About Us

Here at Crapple, we aim to be the leading company in furthering what humans are capable of doing and performing. Crapple was founded by two visionary thinkers, Jeve Stobs and Weve Stozniak-two half-human, half-robotic pioneers in the transhumanist movement. Our mission is to inspire individuals to integrate advanced technologies into their lives, transcending the limitations of the human body and mind.

Whether it's as simple as replacing one limb or adopting an entirely robotic body, we believe this is a step toward a brighter future-one where technology empowers humanity to be its most productive and fulfilled self. At Crapple, we stand for more than enhancement. We stand for freedom, innovation, and living life without boundaries.
